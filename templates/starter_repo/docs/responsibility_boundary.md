# Responsibility Boundary

This starter helps structure reviewable AI-assisted engineering work.

It does not:

- approve a product;
- prove compliance;
- certify safety;
- authorize connector access;
- replace engineering review;
- replace formal ALM, SOP, QMS, legal, security, privacy, or regulatory
  processes.

## Project Owner Responsibilities

The project owner must provide:

- real product facts;
- applicable standards and contractual obligations;
- source and evidence access rights;
- risk classification;
- review and approval roles;
- formal-system entry rules;
- release authority.

## AI Agent Responsibilities

The AI agent may:

- organize candidate requirements, decisions, traces, and evidence;
- draft review prompts;
- identify missing evidence;
- prepare bounded handoff material.

The AI agent must not:

- invent source authority;
- silently promote candidates to records;
- claim formal approval;
- write to formal systems without explicit approved connector authority.
