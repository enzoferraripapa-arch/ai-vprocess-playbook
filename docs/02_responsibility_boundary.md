# Responsibility Boundary

These examples are operating patterns, not permission to ship, certify, or rely
on a product.

If you adapt this playbook, you are responsible for:

- choosing the applicable laws, standards, customer rules, and internal process;
- deciding whether the examples fit your product, risk class, and organization;
- verifying every generated or imported artifact;
- assigning competent reviewers and approvers;
- maintaining official records in your formal system;
- validating tests, evidence, release gates, deployment, rollback, monitoring,
  complaint handling, and support.

## Do Not Treat This Playbook As

- proof of compliance;
- production readiness;
- a safety case;
- a cybersecurity case;
- a privacy assessment;
- legal advice;
- a quality-system record;
- a formal ALM adapter;
- a substitute for competent engineering review;
- authorization to use private or third-party material.

## Practical Rule

Use this repository like a map, not like a permit.

It can help you see which profiles, records, traces, gates, reviews, and
handoff packages may be needed. It cannot make those items true, complete,
approved, safe, or sufficient for your project.
